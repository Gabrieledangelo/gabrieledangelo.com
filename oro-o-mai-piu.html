Oro o mai più

Sometimes the treasure we find is not the one we were searching for.

A man encounters a small mythological creature guarding a pot of gold. What initially appears to be the answer to all his desires soon becomes something unexpected, intimate and deeply human.

01

The story

The pircante is the guardian of a small pot of gold. The man who discovers him imagines wealth, escape and the possibility of changing his life.

But the creature has a desire of his own: to experience the sea.

The journey gradually moves away from greed and becomes a story about wonder, empathy and the unexpected value of helping another being fulfil an apparently impossible dream.

02

The project

Conceived as a short vertical film, Oro o mai più combines live action, visual effects and a digitally created fantasy character.

The project is currently in development, from screenplay and visual design to the construction of the pircante and the preparation of the underwater sequence.

03

Current status

Screenplay completed. Character design and visual development in progress.
