About

Musician, composer, 3D artist and actor.

Born in Sicily in 1967, I have spent my life moving between different forms of expression, always searching for the most honest way to turn an idea into something real.

Music has always been the deepest thread. I write original songs shaped by melodic rock, hard rock and the music that first made me want to pick up a guitar, while continuing to explore new sounds, stories and emotions.

Years of 3D visual art taught me how to build worlds from nothing. Acting later taught me how to step inside them. Each discipline has influenced the others, becoming part of the same creative path.

I do not believe creativity belongs to a single age, profession or moment in life. Some things arrive late. Others arrive exactly when they should.
